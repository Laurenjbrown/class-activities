“Project title” uses Twilio SMS API to assist in the creation of a community collage. Users can get started by texting the number, and follow the prompts and questions that follow. When users respond, the text and images they provide will be displayed on the board for a finite and predetermined amount of time. The goal of this piece is to create an ongoing and ephemeral experience for users to interact with. The themes will change, the amount of data received will change, but hopefully it can provide a constant platform for people to contribute, be seen, and be heard.
